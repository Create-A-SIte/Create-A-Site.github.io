About Create-A-Site

Create-A-Site is an innovative platform that leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence to help anyone generate free, unlimited code for websites they might need.

Founded in 2025, we hope to remain free forever. Even without those pesky ads.

Our Technology

Advanced AI Models

We utilize state-of-the-art language models including DeepCoder, DeepSeek, and Qwen to provide the most accurate and context-aware code generation.

Real-time Preview

See your generated code in action immediately with our integrated preview system that updates as you edit.

Secure & Private

Your code and prompts are processed securely, with options for downloading your code virus-free.

Community Driven

We actively incorporate feedback from our growing community to improve our tools.
